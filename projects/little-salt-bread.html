The Shio Pan Philosophy

Quiet precision,
loud flavors.

Shio Pan, or "Salt Bread," is a study in Japanese baking minimalism. What appears simple is actually a complex orchestration of high-moisture dough and premium cultured butter.

At Little Salt Bread, we use a 48-hour cold fermentation process. This allows the enzymes to break down the starches, resulting in a crumb that is remarkably soft while maintaining a satisfying, chewy pull.

Finished with hand-harvested Malden sea salt, each bun is a tactile journey through texture and taste.
